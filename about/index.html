About

In practice, FluidFS is a research project to create a distributed file system in user space (FUSE) that is highly consistent and reliable. It is meant as a Dropbox replacement, allowing direct synchronization between devices on a personal network rather than going through a cloud service. Strong consistency is provided through a novel approach to distributed coordination called hierarchical consensus.

This research is being conducted in the Computer Science Department at the University of Maryland. All related research and publications will be posted on this page eventually.
